## Context

APraise is an Italian **executive search** boutique working on senior mandates for enterprise clients. The business model is classic executive search: a small number of open roles at any given time, each highly specialised, fees tied to placement seniority, and tight deadlines set by the end client.

APraise's edge depends on its **speed in identifying and qualifying senior profiles**, candidates who are often not actively on the market (passive candidates sourced via LinkedIn, industry events, and personal networks).

## The Real Problem

Executive search has operational characteristics that generic ATS software simply doesn't handle well:

- **Most candidates come through active LinkedIn sourcing**, not inbound applications. Consultants spend hours browsing target profiles, manually copying names, roles, companies, experience, and education into the firm's ATS.
- **JD↔candidate matching is done from memory**, senior consultants who recall "I spoke to Mario Rossi six months ago, he'd be perfect for this." Valuable expertise, but impossible to scale.
- **Fragmented database** split across Excel (active mandates), a legacy ATS (historical records), and LinkedIn (sourcing). Three sources of truth, zero cross-source matching.
- **On enterprise mandates**, a single client can require 150+ candidates managed per role: without efficient tooling, the funnel breaks down.

The operational result: senior consultants, expensive, high-value people who should be talking to clients and candidates, were spending a **significant share of their time on manual data entry**.

## What the AI Agent Did

Soraia built **APraise ATS**, a custom platform combining ultra-fast ingestion with an AI matching agent:

1. **Custom Chrome Web Clipper**, the consultant browses LinkedIn, finds the target profile, clicks the extension: in **1 click**, the profile is captured and sent to the ATS.
2. **Structured parsing AI agent**, the raw profile is processed by an agent that extracts structured data with high accuracy: current role, past experience, years of seniority, skills, languages, education, and industry background.
3. **Enterprise-grade workflow**, management of 150+ candidates per role on a single mandate, with structured pipeline stages (contacted / interview / shortlist / presented / closed).

Senior consultants reclaim hours each day for client and candidate conversations. The operational heavy lifting is handled by the agent.

## Results

**1 click from LinkedIn to enriched database**: what used to take 3–5 minutes of manual copy-paste per profile is now 1 click and a few seconds of review.

**150+ candidates managed per enterprise role**: the executive search funnel now operates at the scale demanded by top-tier clients.

**15+ months in continuous production**: a clear signal of operational robustness and the agent's adaptation to APraise's specific workflows.
